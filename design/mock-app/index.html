Day zero — or day two

Create a workspace, or connect to one that already exists.

Entry branches at the door. Create is three steps — storage, hierarchy and first nodes, target standard — then a milestone checklist that hands off to the owning screens; component assignment and rendering are not wizard steps. Connect is the day-two path: clone or attach, validate, open Fleet — no wizard. This page becomes Settings once the first render exists.

Setup progress

1. Choose how deployment definitions will be stored

Importing generated artifacts back into the model (reverse compilation) is out of scope by decision — it is not an entry path.

Level definitions are ordered metadata. Parentage is implied by position, and instance keys appear only in the graph below.

3. Set the enterprise target standard

Most organizations standardize on one target family for deployments. Per-deployment platform exceptions are supported, but they should be governed and rare.

4. Milestones - the wizard stops here and hands off

Component assignment and rendering happen in the screens that own them, scoped to the node you pick; their empty states do the teaching. Once the first render exists, this page becomes Settings.
